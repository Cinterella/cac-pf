Gryffindor

Godric Gryffindor was one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was known for his bravery and daring personality, traits he highly valued and sought in students for his house. Gryffindor's sword, a goblin-made weapon, is enchanted to appear to any worthy Gryffindor in need. This sword has played a significant role in several key events, including the destruction of Horcruxes in the fight against Voldemort.

Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin was known for his cunning, resourcefulness, and ambition. He valued pure-blood status and believed that magical learning should be kept within all-magical families. His house often attracts students with leadership qualities and a certain ruthlessness. Slytherin's creation of the Chamber of Secrets, which housed a basilisk, was intended to purge the school of Muggle-born students.

Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff was renowned for her kindness, fairness, and loyalty. Unlike the other founders, Helga was inclusive, willing to accept students of all abilities and backgrounds, provided they were hardworking and loyal. She was particularly adept at food-related charms, many of which are still used in the Hogwarts kitchen

Ravenclaw

Rowena Ravenclaw was famed for her intelligence and creativity. She valued learning and wisdom above all, and sought students who shared her passion for knowledge. Her house produced many of the greatest thinkers and innovators in the wizarding world. Famous Characters

Harry Potter

Being one of the most powerful wizards of his generation, Harry was prophesied to defeat, the self-proclaimed dark lord Voldemort, along with the support of his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. After defeating Voldemort, he married his true love, Ginny Weasley and had three children, James, Albus, and Lily

Hermione Granger

She is a muggle-born witch who is initially on the wrong foot with both Harry and Ron until they save her life on Halloween from a Mountain troll. Since then, Hermione has had a strong friendship with Harry and develops romantic feelings for Ron. They also help Harry throughout the school year. After Ron breaks up with Lavender Brown, he and Hermione finally admit their feelings to each other and begin dating. After the war with Voldemort, Hermione and Ron marry and have two children: a girl named Rose and a little boy, Hugo.

Ron Wesley

Ron inherited the typical Weasley family traits: fiery red hair and a freckled complexion. When he became embarrassed, his ears notably turned red. He had blue eyes, a long nose, and was described as being very tall and lanky, with big hands and feet. In this way, his stature was similar to those of his older brothers Bill and Percy, rather than the heavily built Charlie, and Fred and George

Ginny Weasley

The only daughter of Arthur and Molly, the younger sister of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, George and Ron, Ginny is the only girl in the Weasley family. At a young age, she fell in love with the wizard Harry Potter but could never act upon those feelings, until the sixth film/book. Albus Dumbledore

Albus was the first-born son of Kenra and Percival Dumbledore. His younger siblings included a brother, Aberforth and a younger sister, Ariana. At age eighteen, Dumbledore met Gellert Grindelwald. They both created a blood pact to never fight one another, both appearing unbreakable. However, his younger sister, Ariana, was killed in a three-way-duel between Albus and Aberforth and Gellert Grindelwald. Unaware of which spell killed her, Grindelwald fled the scene, which haunted Albus in his older years.

Severus Snape

Severus Snape was born to Tobias Snape and Eileen Prince. Growing up near Lily Potter, Snape and her were best friends but Severus found it hard to live with his parents, as his father hated magic. Severus started at Hogwarts with Lily, where he was Sorted into Slytherin House. This put them in rival houses. Became the immediate enemy of James Potter and Sirius Black and was a frequent victim of their bullying. This led him to be irritable towards James's son Harry when he was a professor.Shortly before Lily Evans was murdered by Lord Voldemort, Snape changed sides and became a member of the Order of the Phoenix as well as a double agent during the Second Wizarding War.
